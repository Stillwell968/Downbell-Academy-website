"Private Shooling"

— Best Education

We offer full time private and home schooling services for Primary and Secondary levels. Our full time students do the International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE)/ Cambridge.

"Computer Aided Learning"

— Learn Technology

Dawn Bell offers computer aided learning to equip students for this digital Era.

"Assessments"

— Quality Education

Once a student is registered Dawn Bell academy offers a pre assessment to determine the kind of help the student need and continue to offer continuous assessments to track progress.

"One on One Tutorial Services"

— Tutoring

We offer tutorial services for PSLE, JCE, BGCSEand IGCSE. We also offer extra lessons, remediation, homework assistance or exam coaching for part time students from other schools or repeating students.

Students who come for extra lessons or tutorials come after school. Prices and duration depend on the number of subjects. The Academy closes at 7pm. For tutorial sessions, a student is given a minimum of an hour and a maximum of 2 hours depending on the subject content or the number of subjects.

"Tutorial Service Times"

— Attendance

1 subject: 2 days a week (2 hours 30 minutes)

2 subjects: 3 days a week (3 hours 30 minutes)

3 subjects: 4 days a week (5 hours 30 minutes)

4 subjects: 5 days a week (6 hours 30 minutes)

5 subjects: 5 days a week (7 hours 30 minutes)

6 or more subjects: 5 days a week (10 hours)
