Dawn Bell was founded by Miss Ndiko Muzila in 2013, who is the CEO of the institution. The Academy has a board and a school management team which consists of the CEO and ten heads of departments.





Miss Ndiko Muzila is an accreduted Trainer and Educator by BQA (Botswana Qualifications Authority) who graduated from the University of Botswana with a Barchelor's Degree in PGDE. She also holds a Master's in Education from UNICAF University. She is the author of 42 books. She has 9 years of experience in teaching and once won two awards as Best Teacher in School and Best Quality producer at philip Moshotle in 2012
